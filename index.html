Our Story

Resvey began in a little room in Nottingham, and was conceived during a masters project on new products development. within months of project completion, the company began offering research services commercially. Resvey is constantly evolving and has become an organization with the vision to operate as a global entity with the sole commitment to doing good and making impact. We use information technology tools, project management methodology and research to achieve our goals. We value fun, commitment and excellence and our company runs with a family mindset among our members. Our mission is to turn simple ideas Into scalable innovative solutions, therefore we look forward to having dedicated project partners and committed members to our family.

“If you are motivated and innovative with a family mindset, then Resvey is the place for you.”

Charles Besidonne, CEO
